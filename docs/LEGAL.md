# Legal And ROM Notes

Cinder64 does not include Nintendo 64 ROM files. You must provide your own lawful ROM backups and comply with the laws that apply to you.

The bundled emulator runtime is vendored under `ThirdParty/gopher64`. Third-party license files remain under that vendored tree.

No reuse license is currently declared for the Cinder64 repository itself. Until the project owner adds an explicit license, do not redistribute, fork for reuse, or incorporate Cinder64 source into another project.
